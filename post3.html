💬 Why I Started This Blog

Posted on May 16, 2025

Sometimes writing helps me process what I’m learning. Other times, it helps me realize how much I’ve already grown. And if someone else finds encouragement through these words, that’s a bonus.

So whether you’re here for tech tips or life thoughts — thank you for reading. This is just the beginning.
