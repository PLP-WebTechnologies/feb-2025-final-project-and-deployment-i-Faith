🙏 Things I’m Grateful For

Posted on May 17, 2025

Gratitude changes everything. Whenever I feel overwhelmed, I try to list a few things I'm thankful for. It brings me back to what really matters.

  • Waking up healthy and safe
  • Learning new things every day
  • The friends who believe in me
  • The freedom to create and express
  • Moments of peace and quiet

It’s easy to focus on what we lack. But there’s so much to appreciate when we pause and look around.

— Cheptoo ✨
