⏳ Growth Takes Time

Posted on May 15, 2025

There are days when I feel like I’m not moving fast enough. Like everyone else is racing ahead while I’m still figuring things out. But then I pause, take a deep breath, and remind myself: growth takes time.

Real growth isn’t loud. It’s the quiet progress that happens when no one is watching. It's the small wins: fixing a bug, understanding a new concept, or even just showing up to learn when you're tired.

If you ever feel like giving up, look back at where you started. You’ll realize you’re already becoming who you wanted to be.

— Cheptoo 🌿
